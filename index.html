Where We Focus & How We Help

Who isn’t going through some sort of digital transformation exercise?

Who hasn’t struggled in getting the results desired?

Three reason account for the nearly 75% so-called “failure rate” of digital transformation. First, it is not pointed in the right direction – e.g., doing more of the same old, same old no longer works given the blurring of industry boundaries and shake up of industry value chains – so, where do you focus to capture new sources of value?

Second, much of the efforts & monies are spent on chasing the hot technologies of the day, or are remedial, focused more on “catching up” with current technologies than on how to get ahead of new market implications.

Third, that “gap” thing – between where you want to go, and how you actually get, or execute. Ever had that problem? Ever not?

The upshot? Lack of clarity and alignment around where to focus (towards new sources of value) and how to execute (given the complexity of new technologies, competitors and changing customer expectations).
