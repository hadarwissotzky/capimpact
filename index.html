Problem

Different industries will be impacted by technology at different rates depending on their mix of capabilities (skill-sets, behavioral patterns and technology assets). These impacts will shape:

  • Where and how different businesses make, and lose, money (e.g., where they play)
  • How effectively (and quickly) they respond to market opportunities, risks and challenges (e.g., how they execute) with material implications on:
    • What organizations do – and the value they deliver
    • What capabilities – including workforce - they need to capture new sources of value.
    • How they orchestrate those capabilities – and those of their partner network.
    • How they make more effective capital allocation decisions under different conditions.

Our Solution

on the new 20%

Our CapImpact Platform provides predictive analytic & role-based tools to help you figure out where to play and how to execute… given our changed competitive environments.

Our secret sauce lies in helping you identify & organize the new 20% of capabilities critical to capture 70% of new value.

4 questions we help you answer:

  • Where is value being created… and destroyed… within your ecosystem and what do you do about it?
  • How, how much and when will (different types of) tech impact your business, and what do you do about it?
  • What will be your resulting Workforce of the Future, and how do you prepare for it?
  • How do you make more effective capital allocation decisions under different conditions / scenarios?
